Using old phone as WiFi router/modem (3/3/24)(day/month/year)

Back (/phones-computers/)

This is my Samsung S10e which is what i use as a modem/router its my spare phone

and its running on Lineage OS 20 (Android 13 Tiramisu) nightly build without GAPPS (No Google Services) and it runs very smooth compared to the original OneUI 4.1 (Android 12 Snow Cone) which was bloated with Google Services, Meta services (Facebook services), Microsoft services, Samsung Services, ETC.. WAY TOO MANY to list and while i was still using OneUI 4.1 i used a tool called Universal Android Debloater but the point is using a plain Android rom like Lineage OS makes a phone much smoother and less bloated

I use my S10e with Proton VPN and have passthrough setup for my wifi hotspot to share it to my other devices.

My S10e usally sits on my bedside table plugged into charge when not used.
